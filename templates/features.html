Our Features

Platform Features

Tools and information to help with crop planning, pricing, and farm management

🌱 Crop Recommendations

Enter your soil test results (nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium levels, pH), along with temperature, humidity, and rainfall data. The system uses a machine learning model trained on agricultural datasets to suggest crops that might suit these conditions.

  • Based on soil nutrient levels
  • Considers temperature and rainfall
  • Trained on crop datasets
  • Instant recommendations

🧪 Fertilizer Recommendations

Select your crop type and enter your soil nutrient levels. The system will recommend appropriate fertilizers and application guidelines based on agricultural data for that crop type.

  • Crop-specific recommendations
  • NPK ratio information
  • Application guidelines
  • Based on soil test results

📊 Market Price Information

View current market prices for various crops from different mandis across India. Compare prices between locations to find potentially better selling opportunities. Price data is updated regularly from available sources.

  • Multiple commodities covered
  • Search by state and district
  • Regular price updates
  • Compare across markets

🤝 Farmer Marketplace

List your crops for sale directly on the platform. The system checks that your listed price is within a reasonable range of current market rates. Buyers can browse listings and contact you directly.

  • List your produce for sale
  • Price validation against market rates
  • Buyers can contact you directly
  • Free to list and browse

🚜 Equipment Sharing

List farm equipment you want to rent out, or browse equipment available for rent in your area. Set your own rental rates and connect with other farmers who need equipment.

  • List equipment for rent
  • Set your own rates
  • Search by location
  • Contact owners directly

📅 Growing Activity Tracker

Keep track of what you're growing and when. Record planting dates, activities, and expenses for each crop. View your timeline and get reminders for upcoming tasks.

  • Track planting and harvest dates
  • Record farming activities
  • Track expenses
  • View your crop timeline

🌤️ Weather Forecasts

Check the weather forecast for your location. View temperature, rainfall predictions, and humidity to help plan your farm activities for the week ahead.

  • Location-based forecasts
  • Rainfall predictions
  • Temperature and humidity
  • Help plan farm activities

📈 Dashboard

Access all features from a single dashboard. View your crops, check market prices, manage your listings, and track your farming activities in one place.

  • All features in one place
  • Easy navigation
  • Quick access to tools
  • Works on mobile devices
