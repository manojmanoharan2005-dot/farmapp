About Smart Farming Assistant

A digital platform to help farmers with crop planning, market information, and farming resources

Our Goal

This platform aims to bring useful farming information together in one place. We want to make it easier for farmers to access crop recommendations, market prices, weather forecasts, and government scheme information without having to search multiple sources.

What We Offer

The platform provides crop suggestions based on soil and weather data, fertilizer recommendations for different crops, current market prices from various mandis, weather forecasts for farm planning, and a marketplace where farmers can list their produce or rent equipment.
